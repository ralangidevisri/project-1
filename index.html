BEAUTIFUL FLOWER

Roses are popular, fragrant, woody perennial shrubs (genus Rosa, family Rosaceae) known for their diverse, multi-petaled flowers, ranging in color from red and white to yellow and orange. Featuring thorny stems and edible petals, they are widely cultivated for beauty, perfumes, and as a symbol of love and passion, with over 300 species

DECORATE FLOWER

Decorate with flowers by using the 60-30-10 color rule, pairing them with complementary vases, and placing them as focal points on coffee tables or in entryways. Enhance spaces with fresh, aromatic blooms like lavender or create lasting displays with sustainable dried flowers or faux arrangements. Utilize greenery like eucalyptus for filler

WATER LILLY

Water lilies are rooted in soil in bodies of water, with leaves and flowers floating on or rising from the surface. Leaves are oval and heart-shaped in Barclaya. Leaves are round, with a radial notch in Nymphaea and Nuphar, but fully circular in Victoria and Euryale.

ELEGANCE FLOWER

"Elegance" in the context of flowers refers to sophisticated,, and often long-lasting floral arrangements or specific, refined blooms chosen for their graceful appearance. These are commonly used for special occasions, gifts, or high-end interior decoration, often featuring a blend of classic and modern aesthetics
